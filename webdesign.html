Web Design

Below are three web designs I completed for school projects. Click on the screenshot to visit the website.

The Sloppy Snowman Boozy Snowball Bar

I created this fictional business page for my University of Florida web principles class in the summer of 2015. The fictional business is a bar that serves alcoholic frozen beverages ("boozy snowballs). The site includes a whimsical logo, custom social icons and other graphics created in Adobe Illustrator, a Jquery-powered animated snowfall background (on the splash page) and horizontal scrolling photo banners (on the drinks and eats pages).

FairWare Equipment Rentals

Here is another fictional business website I created for my UF web principles class. The fictional business is an amusement park equipment rental company. I created the logo in Adobe Illustrator, and the rest of the site was designed via HTML and CSS.

Elaine Broussard's Portfolio Version 1.0

Here is the original design for my portfolio site, which was replaced by the new version you are currently visiting. It was created in the spring of 2014 for my digital portfolio class at Tulane University. Some of the content on this site is outdated, but I decided to preserve it in this form to demonstrate my evolving design skills.
